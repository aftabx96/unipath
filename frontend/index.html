About UniPath

Who we are and what we built.

UniPath

Pakistan Admission Counselor

UniPath is a smart, all-in-one admission workspace built specifically for Pakistani students. Finding the right university can be overwhelming — dozens of institutions, varying merit cutoffs, fee structures, deadlines, and cities to consider all at once.

We built UniPath to simplify that journey. Browse and filter 60+ universities across every province, track application deadlines, compare institutions side by side, and get AI-powered recommendations tailored to your merit and budget — all in one place.

University Explorer Search and filter 60+ universities by city, province, program, merit, and fee.
ML-Powered Predictions A Decision Tree model predicts your admission chances based on your merit percentage.
Deadline Tracker Live countdowns for every admission deadline so you never miss a closing date.
AI Counselor Chat with an AI trained on the university database for instant, contextual advice.
